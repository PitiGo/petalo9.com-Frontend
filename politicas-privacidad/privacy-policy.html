PRIVACY POLICY

Habitly

Last Updated: March 6, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

Thank you for choosing Habitly ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how our App works.

This privacy policy describes:

  • What information our App collects
  • How this information is stored
  • Your rights regarding your data

2. DATA COLLECTION AND STORAGE

2.1 Locally Stored Data

Our App is designed to work completely locally on your device:

  • All data you enter into the App (such as habits, tracking, statistics, or personal notes) is stored exclusively in the local memory of your device.
  • We do not collect, transmit, or process any of your personal data on external servers.
  • We do not send any information over the Internet from the App.

2.2 No User Account

  • You do not need to create an account to use the App.
  • We do not request your name, email, or any other personally identifiable information.

2.3 No Analytics or Tracking

  • We do not use analytics tools to track your behavior within the App.
  • There are no cookies or similar tracking technologies implemented in the App.

3. DEVICE PERMISSIONS

The App may request the following basic permissions necessary for its operation:

  • Storage: To save your habit data locally on your device.
  • Notifications: If you wish to receive reminders for your habits.

All these permissions are optional and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

4. CONTROL OF YOUR DATA

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have complete control over your data at all times.
  • You can delete any stored information by clearing the App's data from your device settings or by uninstalling the App.
  • If you back up your device, your App data may be included in that backup according to your backup system settings.

5. SECURITY

Although we do not process your data on external servers, we care about the security of your information:

  • We recommend keeping your device updated with the latest security patches.
  • We advise using a secure password or locking method for your device if you store personal information in the App.

6. CHANGES TO THE PRIVACY POLICY

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our App is suitable for people of all ages, including children under 13, as we do not collect or process any personal data.
